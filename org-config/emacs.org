** Keep track of the load time

Simple sanity check to see if my configuration is just growing out of control.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp :noweb-ref early-init.el
  (add-hook 'emacs-startup-hook
	  (lambda ()
	    (message "Configuration loaded in %s."
		     (emacs-init-time))))
#+end_src
